+
Myth: "If AI can build a website, my child does not need to learn anything."
Truth. AI builds. Humans decide. AI/SHIP teaches the deciding part, which is the part AI cannot do. Your child learns to direct AI tools, judge their output, talk to real customers, and run a real business. Those skills compound for a lifetime. They do not get replaced.
Q / 02
IS THIS JUST ANOTHER CODING CLASS IN DISGUISE?
+
Myth: "AI/SHIP teaches coding."
Truth. We teach almost no coding. Lovable and Cursor write the code. Your child learns product thinking, customer research, copywriting, design judgement, and selling. The closest analogue is a seven-day MBA, not a coding bootcamp.
